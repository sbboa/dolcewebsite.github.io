ElectroClass Xtra
(600 Hours) Course

The curriculum for students enrolled in the Electrology course consists of 600 hours of combined theoretical and practical (hands-on) instruction, covering all aspects of Electrology. The theoretical component involves online coursework and additional study materials, while practical training refers to the time spent in hands-on classroom sessions. Students may complete the online portion at their own pace; however, quizzes must be taken in person during school hours. The 300 hours of theory/online training comprise 15 modules:

  • History
  • The Human Body
  • The Skin and Its Appendages
  • Ethics
  • Workstation
  • Consultation
  • Hygiene
  • Execution
  • Thermolysis
  • Electrolysis
  • Blend
  • Probes
  • Positioning
  • Marketing
  • Starting Your Business

Practical Hands-on Portion Onsite (320 Hours)

The practical segment of the ElectroClass Xtra is conducted onsite at Dolce Academy.The training begins with an orientation where Dolce Academy will outline the school’s Rules and Regulations, distribute student kits, explain time cards, and cover other essential details related to hands-on training.

During the practical training, students are encouraged to practice on peers and models who come into the academy. It’s advisable to reach out to friends and family who may be interested in serving as models before commencing practical hours.

  • Technique refinement
  • Advanced equipment use
  • Treatment strategy
  • Client management
